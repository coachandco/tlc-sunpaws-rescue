About TLC SunPaws Rescue

TLC SunPaws Rescue began with a single feeding route and the belief that small daily actions create big change. Based in Mexico, our mission is to rescue animals in need — dogs, cats, and every other soul who needs a soft place to land.

Values

  • Compassion for all animals
  • Transparent use of donations
  • Community-led rescue & care

Vision

A healing sanctuary with veterinary partnerships, a grooming center, an on-site rescue shop in Mexico and online (Coach & Co.), volunteer stays, and outreach programs like car care kits to help passersby feed or rescue animals safely.
